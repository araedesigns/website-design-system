About

Alexa Rae is a web designer and project manager based in Philadelphia, PA.

She has 5 years of experience in non-profit management, with expertise in creating inclusive youth programming and grassroots fundraising.

She also has over 1 year of experience in healthcare technology, implementing an oncology decision support software at leading cancer centers nationwide.

Alexa's passions are helping others, building inclusive communities, and learning. She enjoys many hobbies, including trying new restaraunts, traveling, arranging flowers, knitting, singing, volunteering, and more!
